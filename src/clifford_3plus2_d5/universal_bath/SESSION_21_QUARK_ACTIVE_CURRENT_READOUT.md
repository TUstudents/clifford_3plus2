# Session 21 - Quark Active-Current Readout

This session implements the new quark-source ansatz:

$$
\boxed{\text{quark source}=\text{colored active current line }b.}
$$

The lepton source is the selected scalar incidence port $e_1$.  A quark source
is different: it is a colored boundary current.  Session 11 already derived

$$
P_{\rm rad}=P_a,\qquad P_{\rm act}=P_u+P_b.
$$

Inside the active plane, $u$ is the scalar trace.  The unique non-scalar active
line is therefore

$$
b=\frac1{\sqrt2}(0,1,-1).
$$

In residual coordinates $(u,a,b)$:

$$
b=(0,0,1).
$$

## First-Passage Depth

Use the certified oriented repair flag

$$
N=|u\rangle\langle a|+|a\rangle\langle b|.
$$

Starting from the current endpoint $b$:

$$
N^0b=b,\qquad Nb=a,\qquad N^2b=u.
$$

So the first nonzero retarded passage orders are

$$
n(u,a,b)=(2,1,0).
$$

The quark exponents are then read as closure types:

$$

$$

## Up Readout

The up channel is a coherent first-passage amplitude:

$$
F_u=\exp(xN),
\qquad
x=\frac1{\sqrt2}.
$$

Therefore

$$
F_u b=b+xa+\frac{x^2}{2}u
      =b+\frac1{\sqrt2}a+\frac14u.
$$

In light-to-heavy order:

$$
\left(\frac14,\frac1{\sqrt2},1\right).
$$

This recovers the conditional up finite head from a current source.  The
geometric control

$$
\left(\frac12,\frac1{\sqrt2},1\right)
$$

is rejected.

Hermitian current covariance / word-shell readout generated by the same active
current geometry.
